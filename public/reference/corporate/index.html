CORPORATE TRAINING PROGRAMMES

Our training programmes are 100% HRDC claimable and suitable for all levels of fitness, experience, and age. The body, mind and emotions are inter-related. Developing awareness of these linkages and how they feedback on each other is the key to better performance - as a manager and as a person. Our courses can help you to experience the improvements.

#1
Stretching for Executive Wellness by Stretch TherapyTM

Transform your days by developing flexibility, building strength, and alleviate common pains and aches. This programme teaches attendees easy-to-do and highly effective stretching, strengthening and relaxation exercises

See More

This programme provides Stretch Therapy’s comprehensive four-pronged approach with a combination of Stretching & Mobility, Core and Back Strengthening, Postural Re-Patterning, and Relaxation.

  1. Stretching and Mobility - Learn effective stretches to release tensions in muscles, fascia, ligaments and tendons. 
  2. Core and Back Strengthening - Learn exercises to activate inactive muscles and making your posture more efficient. All the exercises will only involve body weight only and engaging the core muscles. 
  3. Postural Re-patterning - Learn exercises to re-pattern long-held postural and movement habits, reducing recurring pain.
  4. Relaxation - The final and often forgotten, is learning to relax. 

The course is grounded in sound anatomy and evidence-based practices and led by an experienced trainer. By using natural body movements to engage the nervous system, participants can achieve deeper stretches in a relaxed state, reaping sustained benefits and effectively relieves stress, tension, and aches. The exercises are practical and accessible for participants of all fitness levels.

Available for half day (4 hours) and full day (7 hours).

#2
Sleep Better, Higher Productivity

This programme empowers participants to enhance both their sleep quality and daily performance through a blend of science-based sleep education, guided relaxation, and practical Stretch Therapy Exercises

See More

Attendees will learn the sleep science effective strategies for improving sleep, develop greater awareness of physical tension, and experience targeted Stretch Therapy exercises designed to increase flexibility, mobility, and overall comfort. 

A sleep routine with 8 exercises will be taught in detail for participants to practice at home.  With continued practice, these techniques help sharpen focus, improve decision-making, and support a calmer, clearer, and more productive mind.

This course is suitable for employees at all levels and is designed to support workplace wellbeing, energy management, and sustainable performance.

Available for half day (4 hours) only

#3
Overcome Neck and Shoulders Pain for Office Workers

This programme coaches participants on stretching and movement exercises that can overcome neck and shoulders pains without medication, treatments and manual manipulation

See More

Using the same 4-pronged approach in the Stretching for Executive Wellness course, the exercises for this course focus on the neck and shoulders areas, a common place where office workers hold their tensions as a result of stress due to tight deadlines, frequent travel, poor office ergonomics, prolonged sitting in misaligned postures, and poor sleep.

The programme balances education with experiential practice, ensuring participants can immediately apply what they learn at both work and home.

#5
C-suite Stretch TherapyTM and Mindfulness Programme

This premium, highly personalised programme is designed specifically for C-suite executives, directors, and senior management. It provides practical tools for both athletic and non-athletic senior executives to sharpen the mind and restore the body, allowing them to make insightful decisions amidst the sustained pressure of leadership.

See More

Delivered one-on-one or in small groups (2–5 participants), this customised training provides deep assessment, targeted mobility work, posture correction, and guided relaxation tailored to each participant’s specific needs.

Programme Overview

Senior executives face sustained pressure, long work hours, extensive travel, and decision-heavy responsibilities. This programme equips leaders with practical tools to:

  • Improve posture and flexibility
  • Reduce chronic bodily pains (neck, shoulders, back, hips, knees, ankles)
  • Enhance sports performance and physical efficiency
  • Improve sleep quality and overall recovery
  • Reduce stress and nervous system overload
  • Build clarity, focus, and long-term leadership resilience

A detailed pre-course questionnaire ensures every session is tailored to individual movement patterns, tension sources, and performance goals.
